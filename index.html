What we do

Flatirons Land Co’s mission is to help people with the process of selling or buying vacant land as fair, convenient and easy as possible, all while avoiding the hassle of the retail market. Flatirons Land Co is family owned and operated and we handle all transactions through reputable title companies or attorneys, ensuring that every purchase is secure and protects the interests of all parties involved. If you have land you want to sell please reach out to us!

We love turning unused land into cash for owners and helping buyers bring their dreams to life with new opportunities

Rural Land

Land located outside of cities and towns, low population density with wide open spaces

Recreational Land

Land used for, camping, fishing, sports and outdoor activities and hunting grounds.

FAQs

Is there a cost for your service?

No! We pay all associated closing costs.

How quickly can you make me an offer and close

We should get you an offer within 1-2 business days and once we receive the signed purchase agreement, we can typically close in about two weeks by a title company or attorney.

Why choose our company?

We have significant experience purchasing and selling vacant land. You don’t need to find a realtor who knows the land market, gets the land ready, wait to sell the property and pay commission and closing costs. We save you time and money!

Owners sell land for many reasons:

  • - Avoiding foreclosure because you’re behind on your taxes.
  • - Having to continue to pay property taxes on a property you aren’t using or will never use.
  • - Owning unattractive vacant land in the middle of nowhere.
  • - Your real estate agent had the property on the market for months and was not selling.
  • - Inheriting a piece of property you will never use
  • - Having an unexpected financial challenge and need to raise capital
